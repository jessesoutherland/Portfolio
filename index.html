About

Hi, my name is Jesse Southerland.
I enjoy pushing myself to grow and learn new things, helping others, and just about any game you can throw at me. A few years ago I had the honor of becoming a father to a beautiful baby girl, which caused me to re-evaluate my long-term goals and what I really wanted out of life. That started me on a journey that led to the wonderful world of coding.
After I completed an immersive, project-based software development bootcamp, I found a new passion with endless growth capabilities.
